Products


Broiler Chicken
Broiler chickens are raised primarily for meat rather than to lay eggs. These poultry are often white and are bred to be large and very healthy, often with more breast meat for the consumer market.We select fresh hens from the farms and provides it in our market.
Country Chicken
Country chicken have feathers in different colours, and mostly graze in the open. The meat is slightly tougher than white poultry meat, but when cooked, is juicy and soaks in spices beautifully.Original Country chicken that are bred by local breeders are available here which we withering and provide it clear,fresh and neat.
Legpiece
Fresh & raw whole leg pieces.It is draft free and hygienically fresher chicken stuffs that increases your taste buds and prepares your cooking easy.Buy some legpieces and fulfill your chicken feast.Best for chicken biryani,tandoori,chicken 65,gravy.
Boneless
Skinless boneless chicken breast has been skinned and deboned.The chicken breast is a particularly healthy part of this already nutritious bird, as it is low in fat and a good source of protein.Enjoy eating your meat without any disturbance of bones.Suits best for chicken 65 ,dragon chicken recipe and other hotel style recipies.
Lollipop
The name chicken lollipop is a nod to how the chicken wings look after trimming.Chicken lollipop is an appetiser popular in Indian cuisine. Chicken lollipop is, essentially a frenched chicken winglet, wherein the meat is cut loose from the bone end and pushed down creating a lollipop appearance.
Skinless Chicken
Some people don't like the chicken with it's skin flesh.For this,We also remove it and provides to the customers.Enjoy your meat at which type you want.
